Investment Scorecard

Tools·Updated 27 Jun 2026·6 min·Confidence: Medium

Weighted scoring framework for cities, micro-locations, buildings and units.

Score Matrix

Score layers

Macro city, micro-location, building, unit, financing, operations and exit risk.

Avoid false precision

A score is a decision aid, not a substitute for judgment. It makes disagreement visible.

Use case

S17 would score high on view and uniqueness, lower on immediate liquidity impact.

Future use
