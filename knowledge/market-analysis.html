Market Analysis

Knowledge·Updated 27 Jun 2026·10 min·Confidence: Medium

Market screening for short-term rental demand, resale strength and future urban development.

Knowledge

Evidence hierarchy

Actual platform history is stronger than claims. Remigiusz’s Booking records mattered because they showed real seasonality and demand.

Development narrative

Urban growth stories can be powerful but dangerous. Młode Miasto was a development thesis; S17 was an existing high-floor view thesis.

Comparable listings

Otodom comparables showed that similar raw-state studios in lower floors could be priced near S17, supporting the idea that the 17th-floor view had value.

Seasonality

Baltic beach demand is seasonal, but stadium events, city tourism and long-term winter tenants can smooth the model.
