IT-GOA-001 — Genova Candidate

Case Studies·Updated 27 Jun 2026·7 min·Confidence: Low

Placeholder dossier for the next research phase: Genova.

Genova Italy Future

Starting thesis

Genova may offer a better first-investment structure because fully furnished apartments around 100k EUR can reduce capital binding and start income sooner.

Key takeaway. This section is designed as an operational reference, not a motivational note. Every claim should eventually map to a document, observation or calculation.

Key constraint

Local physical management is mandatory. Without a reliable cleaning/repair/key partner, the ROI is theoretical.

Research questions

Which districts have legal, safe and guest-friendly short-term rental demand? What does management cost? What building risks dominate older stock?

Decision standard

Genova must beat Gdańsk not by romance, but by risk-adjusted return after management fees and lower capital concentration.
