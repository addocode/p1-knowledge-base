Lexicon·Updated 27 Jun 2026·6 min·Confidence: High

Alphabetical and thematic dictionary of official and internal real-estate terms.

Lexicon

Purpose

The lexicon gives serious definitions to both official terms and internal P1 vocabulary. English is primary, with German or Polish equivalents in italics where useful.

Use

Terms are linked from articles, case studies and templates. Internal concepts such as Porto Style or Prestige 1 are treated with the same rigor as legal or financial vocabulary.

Categories

Finance, law, Polish transaction terms, rental operations, design, risk and internal P1 methodology.

Editorial rule

Definitions should be concise, neutral and reusable. Personal anecdotes belong in case studies, not term definitions.
