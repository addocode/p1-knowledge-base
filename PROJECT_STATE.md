# Mission

Build the highest-quality knowledge platform for international real estate investing.

The objective is not to become the largest real estate website, but the most useful, trustworthy and practical knowledge base for private and professional investors.

The platform combines:

- Academic-quality articles
- Real-world case studies
- Interactive investment tools
- Due diligence workflows
- Investment philosophy
- AI-assisted knowledge management

The project should evolve into a long-term European investment platform.

---

# Core Principles

Quality over quantity.

Every article should be understandable for beginners while still providing value for experienced investors.

Every topic should combine:

- theory
- practical examples
- P1 experiences
- international comparisons
- references
- investment insights

English is the primary language.

Whenever appropriate, preserve original terminology from local markets:

Examples:

- Czynsz
- Księga Wieczysta
- Nuda Proprietà
- Usufrutto
- Grundbuch
